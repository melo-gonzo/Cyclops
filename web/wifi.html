Standalone mode

The camera always hosts its own hotspot when it can't reach a saved network (after ~45s), so it is never unreachable. Standalone mode makes that permanent — useful in hotels, cars, or anywhere without usable WiFi. Connect your phone to (password ) and browse to http://192.168.8.1. Note: no internet on the camera means no NTP — the clock is set automatically from your browser when you open this page.

Saved networks

Strongest saved network wins at boot. The home network keeps its static IP; everything else uses DHCP — when the IP is unknown, try the device's <name>.local (set in Device settings below).
